Get Involved

Our campaigns are powered by dedicated individuals from around the world who come together to support freedom, equality, and justice for Palestine. There are many ways you can contribute to this movement, whether by becoming a volunteer, joining our campaigns to raise awareness, getting involved in local initiatives, or affiliating your organization with our cause. You can also attend our events to stay informed and engaged or make a donation to help provide essential aid, education, and relief to those in need. Every action, no matter how small, brings us one step closer to a just and free Palestine.
